Attenborough Portrait...
Commissions




A portrait of Lord Attenborough for the University of Sussex made from the names of philanthropic donors to the new Attenborough Centre for the Creative Arts in Brighton.

Painting on Canvas. 160cm x 120cm.

Steven Spielberg, Michael Douglas, Denzel Washington,
Martin Scorsese, Robert Downey Jr, Dame Judi Dench,
Sir Ben Kingsley, Dame Maggie Smith,
 are just some of the great people who have made donations
to the project - and as such are names that
feature in the painting.

For more information please visit the University of Sussex page.

Email me for commission enquiries: mike@mikeedwards.co.uk
