'GEORGE MICHAEL'

A portrait of legendary icon George Michael made solely from a readable mix of random snippets of lyrics from George Michael and Wham's most popular songs. The golden glow of the artwork reflecting the warmth of Wham's heyday with songs like
'Club Tropicana' - whilst the cool thoughtful expression hints at George Michael's later work which included themes supporting human rights.

Signed and numbered edition of 50.

Printed on Somerset Velvet 330gsm paper with deckled edges. Certificate of authenticity on request.
